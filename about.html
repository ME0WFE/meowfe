About Us

We’re a cat café dedicated to rescuing and rehoming cats. Enjoy our cozy space while helping us find loving homes for our feline friends. Adopt a cat and make a difference today!

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a welcoming and relaxing environment where people can enjoy spending time with a variety of cat breeds.

Our Market

Our primary market includes cat lovers and enthusiasts, as well as individuals seeking a unique and relaxing café experience in Tel Aviv. We are inspired by successful cat cafés in Saigon, Vietnam, and aim to offer something distinct in the local market.

Why is Meowfe Different?

Meowfe will stand out from other cat cafés in the area by:

  • Featuring a diverse selection of cat breeds.
  • Providing a feeding option for the cats to ensure their well-being.
  • Offering a simple grab-and-go snack rack rather than heavy sit-down meals.
